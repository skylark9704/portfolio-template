Silk Illusions Vintage Finish

A water-based interior texture emulsion for interior puttied/POP/wooden surfaces. Vintage Finish is a water based interior texture emulsion that can be used on interior puttied/POP/wooden surfaces. This material is thick and can be applied using Steel Trowel followed by other tools to make unique patterns. Specific recommended quality of colourants needs to be added for the desired Vintage shade. Silk Illusions Design Metallica can be used as a top coat to get the desired look in some of the Vintage Finish
